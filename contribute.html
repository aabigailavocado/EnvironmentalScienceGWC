Ways to Help

Short Term Solutions


1. Spread awareness to people that use plastic products or use single-use plastics daily.

2. Cut back on seafood consumption.

Long Term Solutions


1. Reduce the amount of plastics used by switching to wood, glass and bamboo.

2. Cook more meals to decrease the amount of plastic bags and containers collected from restaurants and cafes.

3. Buy in bulk- not single-sized/travel-sized items.

4. Purchase items second-hand.
